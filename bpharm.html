Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm)

A 4-year undergraduate program designed to prepare students for careers in pharmaceutical sciences, drug development, and healthcare.

Program Overview

The Bachelor of Pharmacy (B.Pharm) program provides comprehensive education in pharmaceutical sciences, combining theoretical knowledge with practical skills in drug formulation, pharmacology, medicinal chemistry, and pharmacy practice.

Our program is approved by the Pharmacy Council of India (PCI) and prepares graduates for diverse career paths in the pharmaceutical industry, hospitals, research, and regulatory affairs.

Program Highlights

  • Duration: 4 years (8 semesters)
  • Eligibility: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology/Mathematics
  • Total Credits: 208
  • Industry-oriented curriculum with hands-on training
  • Modern laboratories and research facilities
  • Mandatory internship program
  • Placement assistance with top pharmaceutical companies

Curriculum Structure

The B.Pharm curriculum covers both core and elective subjects across pharmaceutical sciences:

Core Subjects

  • Pharmaceutics
  • Pharmacology
  • Pharmaceutical Chemistry
  • Pharmacognosy
  • Pharmaceutical Analysis
  • Biochemistry
  • Clinical Pharmacy
  • Biopharmaceutics

Elective Subjects (Year 3 & 4)

  • Industrial Pharmacy
  • Pharmaceutical Biotechnology
  • Clinical Research
  • Pharmacy Practice
  • Pharmaceutical Marketing

Career Opportunities

Graduates can pursue diverse roles including:

  • Community Pharmacist
  • Hospital Pharmacist
  • Pharmaceutical Researcher
  • Drug Inspector
  • Medical Representative
  • Quality Control/Assurance Manager
  • Regulatory Affairs Officer
